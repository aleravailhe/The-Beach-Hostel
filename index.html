Founded in 1993, our principles and values are based on the Buddhist culture in which it has as a precept the search for the end of human suffering and attain spiritual enlightenment, our hostel offers guests an unique experience of connection between soul, mind and nature.

Our bedrooms are based in Indian culture and architecture, so they have a lot of space for you feel comfortable and well. We offer three types of accommodations: Standard room, Nature room and Private room.

With a unique and cozy design, our livingroom has a relaxing music producing a calm environment for breakfast, reading a book, meditating or to work. We have a lot of books that you can read, Wi-fi connection and some snacks.

For a good life style, we need to add good things like good food. To start a beautiful and calm day you need to start with a healthy and delicious breakfast. So, we offer a veggie breakfast that you'll never forget.
